设计哲学
------------------
* Highly extensible
* Avoid premature optimization
* In favor of a simpler, less-cluttered grammar

::

  Beautiful is better than ugly
  Explicit is better than implicit
  Simple is better than complex
  Complex is better than complicated
  Readability counts
                    -- the Zen of Python
